A ticket must be included in a weekly bucket if it was open at any time during that week.

A ticket counts for a week when:
opened_at is before the end of the week
and
closed_at is null or closed_at is on or after the start of the week

Include tickets opened and closed within the same week

effective_end = closed_at if the ticket closed during that week

Then:
open_age_hours = effective_end - opened_at

Do not only use tickets still open at the end of the week.
